About

Our History

Established in 1999, Dazium Design Solutions lures clients from all over the United States, but we call the heartland our home. Our offices are located in the historic Livestock Exchange building in the West Bottoms area of Kansas City proper. Dazium Design Solutions is marketing materials, graphic design, web development and project management. We have the capability, experience, and resources to work with clients from an individual to a large corporation.

Our Style

We have built a business helping our clients with effective design, relevant websites, custom web apps and a vast array of marketing materials. We are masters of our craft and undaunted by custom solutions, tight timelines or limited budgets. We have no problem working with unorthodox ideas, limited budgets, and any other unique parameters. The combination of experience and our nimble size results in cost-effective production, speed to market, simple solutions, and innovative processes.

We will work with you to ensure your satisfaction. We have high standards for the products we design and will work on a project until it meets those standards. We are an experienced group of graphic designers, production art specialists, content designers, and web engineers.
